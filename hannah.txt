Hannah is a charmingly chunky brown dog with a demeanor as warm as her coffee-colored fur. Despite her slightly plump frame, she carries herself with an endearing waddle that only adds to her appeal. Her eyes, always gleaming with a gentle kindness, seem to smile at everyone she meets. Hannah spends her days lounging in sunny spots around the house or ambling through the neighborhood, where she's a familiar and beloved figure. Her love for treats is matched only by her affection for cuddles, making her a favorite among both children and adults in her family.
